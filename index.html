About Gargee Global

Gargee Global Computer Institute is a professionally governed IT education institution committed to transforming students into industry-ready professionals. Located in Surajpur, Greater Noida, the institute follows structured academic delivery models aligned with college-level standards and global industry requirements. Our programs are designed after in-depth market research, corporate demand analysis, and practical exposure frameworks.

Academic Affiliation & Quality Assurance

Gargee Global Computer Institute operates in academic association with Fucture IT College, ensuring curriculum alignment, structured academic progression, and certification pathways that reflect higher education standards.
